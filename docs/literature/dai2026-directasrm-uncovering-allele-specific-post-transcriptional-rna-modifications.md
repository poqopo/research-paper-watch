# DirectASRM: Uncovering allele-specific post-transcriptional RNA modifications through direct RNA sequencing

## Healthcare relevance

Supports transcript-level interpretation of allele-specific RNA modifications and regulatory variation.

## Analysis

- Abstract: SUMMARY: We developed DirectASRM, a comprehensive database for the systematic identification, integration, and annotation of allele-specific RNA modifications (ASRMs) from direct RNA sequencing data.  DirectASRM enables single-base, transcript-level detection of ASRMs across multiple RNA modification types, diverse organisms and condition-specific contexts.  The database further evaluates the confidence of each ASRM-SNP pair association within isoform context by jointly considering statistical evidence of allelic modification imbalance and independent support from external next-generation sequencing (NGS) - based RNA modification resources.
