# Multi-cancer early detection test in symptomatic patients referred for cancer investigation in England and Wales (SYMPLIFY)

## Healthcare relevance

SYMPLIFY broadens MCED from population screening to triage of patients already referred for cancer workup. That distinction matters because positive predictive value, urgency, and downstream testing are different in symptomatic care.

- Abstract: methylation MCED tested in symptomatic referrals
- Limitation: screening evidence did not cover diagnostic triage
- Solution: blinded assay compared with standard-care outcomes
